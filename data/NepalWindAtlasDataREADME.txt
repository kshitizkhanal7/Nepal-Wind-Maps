This is a short description of the  files in this directory.

The data is for the numerical wind atlas for Nepal is given

.ASC - a ERSI compatible format 

All the data is given at 5 km resolution.

------------------------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------------------

AtlasDataNEP05_1_1_uni_z50.5.wrm.u_m.ASC

The above files contain the simulated annual mean wind speed [m/s] at 50 m a.g.l. over the terrain as described in the mesoscale model.

------------------------------------------------------------
------------------------------------------------------------

AtlasDataNEP05_1_1_uni_z50.5.wrm.e_m.ASC

The above files contain the simulated annual mean wind power density [W/m^2] at 50 m a.g.l. over the terrain as described in the mesoscale model.

------------------------------------------------------------
------------------------------------------------------------

AtlasDataNEP05_1_1_uni_z0.5.wam.u_m.ASC

The above files contain the generalize annual mean wind speed [m/s] at 50 m a.g.l. over the flat terrain and uniform roughness of 0.03 m.

------------------------------------------------------------
------------------------------------------------------------


AtlasDataNEP05_1_1_uni_z0.5.wam.e_m.ASC

The above files contain the generalized annual mean wind power density [W/m^2] at 50 m a.g.l. over the flat terrain and uniform roughness of 0.03 m.


------------------------------------------------------------
------------------------------------------------------------


The files were created using the KAMM/WAsP methodology of Risø National Laboratory, DTU, Denmark.
